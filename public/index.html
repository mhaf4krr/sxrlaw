Why Srinagar Journal

Srinagar Journal serves as an important reference tool to identify, retrieve and document important cases and decisions of J&K High Court and Supreme Court. It also catalogues important Statutes issued by various Statutory bodies of the State and the country and their amendments/modifications/revisions, if any. It has also a section of select papers discussing current and burning themes triggering state and national law and society.
